7-Day Gluten-Free Meal Plan

Day 3

Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Tomatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1 small tomato (diced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add spinach and tomato, and cook until spinach is wilted.
  3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan and scramble until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot.

Shopping List:

  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Tomato
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Lunch: Tuna Salad with Mixed Greens and Olive Oil Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 can tuna (drained)
  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup red onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Add the drained tuna on top.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Serve immediately.

Shopping List:

  • Tuna
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Red onion
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Dinner: Stir-Fried Tofu with Bell Peppers and Zucchini

Ingredients:

  • 150g firm tofu (cubed)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (gluten-free)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  3. Add the cubed tofu and cook until golden brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add bell pepper and zucchini slices to the pan, and stir-fry for another 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, and stir to combine.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Serve hot.

Shopping List:

  • Firm tofu
  • Bell pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Soy sauce (gluten-free)
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
