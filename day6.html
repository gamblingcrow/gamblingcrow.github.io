7-Day Gluten-Free Meal Plan

Day 6

Breakfast: Oatmeal made with Almond Milk Topped with Sliced Almonds and Blueberries

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup gluten-free oats
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/4 cup blueberries
  • 1 tbsp sliced almonds

Instructions:

  1. Combine oats and almond milk in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until oats are tender and have absorbed the milk.
  3. Top with blueberries and sliced almonds.
  4. Serve hot.

Shopping List:

  • Gluten-free oats
  • Almond milk
  • Blueberries
  • Sliced almonds

Lunch: Chickpea Salad with Cucumbers, Red Onions, and Lemon-Tahini Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup canned chickpeas (rinsed and drained)
  • 1/2 cup cucumbers (diced)
  • 1/4 cup red onions (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumbers, and red onions.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the chickpea salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve immediately or chill before serving.

Shopping List:

  • Canned chickpeas
  • Cucumbers
  • Red onions
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Dinner: Grilled Lamb Chops with Zucchini Noodles

Ingredients:

  • 2 lamb chops
  • 2 medium zucchinis (spiralized into noodles)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the lamb chops with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the lamb chops for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. While the lamb chops are grilling, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and saut√© for 1 minute.
  6. Add zucchini noodles and cook for 3-4 minutes, until tender.
  7. Serve the grilled lamb chops with zucchini noodles.

Shopping List:

  • Lamb chops
  • Zucchinis
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper

Snack: Sliced Cucumber with Tzatziki

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup tzatziki

Instructions:

  1. Slice the cucumber.
  2. Serve with tzatziki for dipping.

Shopping List:

  • Cucumber
  • Tzatziki
