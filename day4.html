7-Day Gluten-Free Meal Plan

Day 4

Breakfast: Cottage Cheese with Pineapple Chunks

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (fresh or canned in juice, drained)

Instructions:

  1. Spoon the cottage cheese into a bowl.
  2. Top with pineapple chunks.
  3. Enjoy!

Shopping List:

  • Cottage cheese
  • Pineapple chunks

Lunch: Gluten-Free Wrap with Roast Beef, Lettuce, and Mustard

Ingredients:

  • 1 gluten-free wrap
  • 100g roast beef (sliced)
  • 2 large lettuce leaves
  • 1 tbsp mustard

Instructions:

  1. Lay the gluten-free wrap on a flat surface.
  2. Spread mustard over the wrap.
  3. Place roast beef slices and lettuce leaves on top.
  4. Roll up the wrap and serve.

Shopping List:

  • Gluten-free wrap
  • Roast beef
  • Lettuce
  • Mustard

Dinner: Grilled Pork Chop with Green Beans

Ingredients:

  • 150g pork chop
  • 1 cup green beans (trimmed)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the pork chop with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the pork chop for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. While the pork chop is grilling, steam the green beans for about 5-7 minutes until tender.
  5. Serve the pork chop with green beans on the side.

Shopping List:

  • Pork chop
  • Green beans
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Snack: Celery Sticks with Peanut Butter

Ingredients:

  • 2 large celery stalks (cut into sticks)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Cut the celery stalks into sticks.
  2. Serve with peanut butter for dipping.

Shopping List:

  • Celery
  • Peanut butter
