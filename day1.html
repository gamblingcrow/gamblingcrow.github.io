7-Day Gluten-Free Meal Plan

Day 1

Breakfast: Greek Yogurt with a Handful of Berries

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Instructions:

  1. Spoon the Greek yogurt into a bowl.
  2. Top with mixed berries.
  3. Enjoy!

Shopping List:

  • Plain Greek yogurt
  • Mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)

Lunch: Baked Salmon with Steamed Broccoli

Ingredients:

  • 150g salmon fillet
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedge (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place salmon fillet on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the salmon, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until salmon is cooked through.
  5. While the salmon is baking, steam the broccoli florets for about 5-7 minutes until tender.
  6. Serve the salmon with the steamed broccoli and a lemon wedge on the side.

Shopping List:

  • Salmon fillet
  • Broccoli
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon

Dinner: Beef Stir-Fry with Bell Peppers and Snap Peas

Ingredients:

  • 150g lean beef (sliced thinly)
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (gluten-free)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add the sliced beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Add snap peas and bell pepper slices to the pan, and stir-fry for another 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, and stir to combine.
  6. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
  7. Serve hot.

Shopping List:

  • Lean beef
  • Snap peas
  • Bell pepper
  • Soy sauce (gluten-free)
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Olive oil

Snack: Carrot Sticks with Hummus

Ingredients:

  • 1 large carrot (cut into sticks)
  • 1/4 cup hummus

Instructions:

  1. Cut the carrot into sticks.
  2. Serve with hummus on the side for dipping.

Shopping List:

  • Carrot
  • Hummus
