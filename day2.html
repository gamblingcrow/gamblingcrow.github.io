7-Day Gluten-Free Meal Plan

Day 2

Breakfast: Smoothie with Almond Milk, Spinach, and a Banana

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 handful spinach
  • 1 banana

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy!

Shopping List:

  • Almond milk
  • Spinach
  • Banana

Lunch: Turkey Lettuce Wraps with Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 100g cooked turkey breast (sliced)
  • 4 large lettuce leaves
  • 1/2 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 small tomato (diced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place turkey slices on lettuce leaves.
  2. Top with avocado slices and diced tomato.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Wrap the lettuce around the fillings and serve.

Shopping List:

  • Turkey breast
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Tomato
  • Salt and pepper

Dinner: Quinoa Salad with Cucumbers, Tomatoes, and Feta Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup quinoa (cooked)
  • 1/2 cup cucumbers (diced)
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cucumbers, tomatoes, and feta cheese.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the quinoa salad and toss to combine.
  4. Serve chilled or at room temperature.

Shopping List:

  • Quinoa
  • Cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Snack: Apple Slices with Almond Butter

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp almond butter

Instructions:

  1. Slice the apple.
  2. Serve with almond butter for dipping.

Shopping List:

  • Apple
  • Almond butter
